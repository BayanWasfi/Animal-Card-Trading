Vampire Bats

Bats, sometimes called flying foxes, are nocturnal (active at night).

  • Scientific Name:: Chiroptera
  • Wing Size:: 15cm to 1.7m
  • Lifespan:: 20 years
  • Habitat: Live in caves

Bats see in the dark using speciall skill called echolocation. Bats make noises and wait for the sound waves to bounce back off objects (an echo), if it doesn't bounce back then they can safely fow forward. They can tell the distance of various objects by how quickly the sound waves bounce back to them.
